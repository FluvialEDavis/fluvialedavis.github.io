Current Projects

Ecohydrologic Modeling - Ramuschaka Watershed

Interdisciplinary (geology and engineering) and cross-cultural project to quantify water storage in a rural Peruvian watershed and design canal improvements for local agricultural use. My contribution is focused on utilizing imagery from UAVs (drones) to create high resolution digital elevation models and orthomosaic photos for use in various ecohydrologic models.
Project funded by Geoscientists Without Borders.
